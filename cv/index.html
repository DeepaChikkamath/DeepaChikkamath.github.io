  • 05/2024 - 07/2024
    Charpak Lab Scholar
    Observatoire de Nice Côte d'Azur
    • I worked under the guidance of Prof. Giorgio Krstulovic at the Observatoire de Nice Côte d'Azur, France, from May 2024 to July 2024, with the Charpak Scholarship. I worked on understanding the role of compressibility in turbulent fluctuations in a two-dimensional fluid model (The Navier-Stokes equation). The results we found will be compared to the ones found for quantum turbulence (The Gross-Pitaevskii equation) in two dimensions. Learnt the theory of turbulence and used Matlab for 2D incompressible and compressible fluid models and calculated energy cascades, circulation, palinstrophy and enstrophy.

I learned numerical simulations on Basilisk, an open-source Navier Stokes Equation solver (successor of Gerris), the theory of fluid dynamics, computational fluid dynamics, drop dynamics.
